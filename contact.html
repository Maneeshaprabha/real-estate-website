FAQ

how to cancel booking?

If you need to cancel a booking, you can usually do so through the same platform or website where you made the reservation. Look for the "cancel" or "modify" button, and follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process. Be sure to check the cancellation policy, as there may be fees or penalties for cancelling within a certain timeframe. If you have any trouble cancelling your booking, you can usually contact customer support for assistance.

How can I schedule a viewing for a property?

To schedule a viewing, simply contact our real estate agency and speak to one of our agents. They will be able to provide you with available viewing times and arrange a time that suits your schedule. We recommend scheduling a viewing as soon as possible to avoid missing out on the property you are interested in.

How can I pay for this?

You can pay for our services through various payment methods such as credit card, debit card, online payment systems, bank transfer, or cash payment, depending on the options available in your region. Once you have selected the property or service you wish to purchase, our website will guide you through the payment process and provide you with the necessary payment options. If you encounter any issues or have questions about the payment process, you can contact our customer support team for assistance.

how to contact with the buyers?

To contact buyers interested in your property, you can provide your contact information through your real estate agent or through your listing on property websites. Buyers may also reach out to you directly through the contact information provided in the listing. It's important to respond to any inquiries in a timely manner to ensure a smooth communication and negotiation process.

Can I buy a property without physically visiting it?

While it's always recommended to physically visit a property before buying it, it is possible to buy a property without visiting it. However, it's important to work with a trusted real estate agent who can provide you with accurate information about the property and the area before making a decision to buy.

Can I request a viewing of a property before making an offer?

Yes, you can request a viewing of a property by contacting the real estate agency or agent handling the listing. They will arrange a suitable time for you to visit the property and view it in person before making an offer.
